Continue

  • To proceed with the previously selected rout press "continue". When the selected rout appears on the screen a selection bar appears above the map, showing various buttons to select.

  • Press to activate the help menu.

  • Press to toggle view between map/satellite view.

  • Press to display overlays of various services and places.

  • Press to activate the settings menu

  • Press to display the next rout

  • Press to zoom in on the map.

  • Press to zoom out of the map.
